### 🌌 What I do

</div>

Most people write software. I try to make software disappear into products that feel effortless, and into automations that make busywork someone else's problem.

**Aynam.ai** is the product side: an AI platform helping people become confident speakers, built in public, one imperfect version at a time.
The automation side is quieter AI agents that step into a business's repetitive workflows and handle it. No hand-holding required.

Different problems. Same obsession: build the thing so well it stops needing you.
